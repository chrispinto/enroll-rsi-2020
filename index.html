Running Start

Through Running Start, high school juniors and seniors can attend SPSCC tuition-free and earn college credits—or even an AA degree—at the same time as their high school diploma.

Each year, hundreds of Running Start grads transfer to a 4-year university in Washington as a junior with an associate degree, saving more than $8,000 toward a bachelor’s degree.

Your student can experience a different learning environment, be more independent, and set themself up for a smooth transition to a university. Fee Waivers and Book Loans are available to qualifying students.
